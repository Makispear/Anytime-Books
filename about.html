About Anytime Books

Description

Anytime books is designed for anyone, 'anytime' who prefers spending their leisure time relaxing with a book and not wasting time on research and decision making. You may choose a topic of interest and will be provided with a list of books that are on the current week's Best-Seller List.


Review the book's Title, Author, and a brief Description. You may then save any selection to your Favorites, with a 'click'. They will be listed on the side of page, with the ISBN. You will always remember the book and have information at your fingertips. Never forget again!
